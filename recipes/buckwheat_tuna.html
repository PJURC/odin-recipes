Buckwheat with Tuna

Description

A simple buckwheat and tuna recipe. Great macros and fast to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of buckwheat
  • 1 can of tuna in own juice
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Paprika or other seasoning mix
  • Green Pesto sauce

Steps

  1. Heat pot with water at maximum heat for 10 minutes. When boiling, add buckwheat and boil for 20 minutes on medium heat.
  2. Take out the bag and mix the buckwheat with tuna.
  3. Mix with Pesto sauce, olive oil, spice mix and serve hot.
