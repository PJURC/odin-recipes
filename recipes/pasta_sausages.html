Buckwheat with Tuna

Description

A simple pasta with sausages recipe. Great macros and fast to make.

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta
  • Three sausages
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Paprika or other seasoning mix
  • Red Pesto sauce

Steps

  1. Heat pot with water at maximum heat for 10 minutes. When boiling, add pasta and boil for 10 minutes on medium heat.
  2. While pasta is cooking, chop sausages and fry them on a heated frying pan for 5 minutes.
  3. Take out the pasta and mix with cooked sausages.
  4. Mix with Pesto sauce, olive oil, spice mix and serve hot.
